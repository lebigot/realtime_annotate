# Annotations for music pieces:

# Convention for these annotations: the meanings are chosen so that
# annotations with no explicit associated value have by convention a
# medium/standard/default value.

s    start of a piece (silence before the start of a piece)
e    end of a piece (silence after the end of a piece)
u    uninspired (0 = a little, 2 = very much)
g    glitch [technical, or few uninspired notes] (0 = small, 2 = major)
i    inspired (0 = somewhat, 2 = nicely)
b    break in the piece, that could be the start or end of a piece
